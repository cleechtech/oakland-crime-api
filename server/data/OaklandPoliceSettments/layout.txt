CITY: City Attorney case number.
DISTRICT: Federal court case number.
SUPERIOR: Superior court case number.
OFFICER: Name of officer or officers, comma separated, formatted First Middle Last or First Initial Last or Last Only.

DATE: Date filed in court, per City Attorney's case report or court records.
SETTLEMENT: Settlement amount rounded to decimal point.
SUMMARY: Summary of allegations. See readme.txt file for full explanation.
PATTORNEY: Plaintiff attorney or attorneys per the City Attorney case report or court records.
